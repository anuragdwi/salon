About Us

Our Philosophy

We are the leader in beauty and hair. We guarantee the highest quality service to our clients. We provide the most current fashion through a highly trained, professional staff in a comfortable, clean, and friendly environment. We are uncompromising in maintaining our high standards of quality. We are consistent in our effort to build an always-improving employee and management team.

Our Goal

We are taking every opportunity to identify and satisfy the needs of our clients and staff. We understand that everything we do must be designed to satisfy clients. Together we are creating an environment that is based on mutual trust and respect. We strive to make Amaci Salon an environment where everyone is acknowledged, recognized, and appreciated.
